About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Ocean University of China, supervised by Professor Junyu Dong. My research focuses on computer vision and deep learning, with particular interests in stereo matching, semantic segmentation, object detection, and cross-domain adaptation.

Research Interests: Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Stereo Matching, Semantic Segmentation, Domain Adaptation
