What We Do

We provide an advanced platform for underwater 3D reconstruction and navigation. Our system enables autonomous underwater vehicles (AUVs) and remotely operated vehicles (ROVs) to build accurate 3D maps of underwater environments in real-time, even in challenging conditions with low visibility and complex lighting.

Key Innovation: Combining state-of-the-art visual SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) with underwater-optimized stereo vision algorithms, our platform delivers reliable 3D reconstruction capabilities specifically designed for the unique optical challenges of subsea environments.

Why Choose Our Platform

Designed for Underwater Challenges

Traditional 3D reconstruction systems fail underwater due to unique optical phenomena. Our platform is specifically engineered to overcome these challenges:

  • Handles wavelength-dependent light attenuation and color distortion
  • Robust to backscattering from suspended particles
  • Compensates for refraction through camera housings
  • Works in low-light and high-turbidity conditions

Easy Integration

Simple RESTful API allows seamless integration with existing ROV systems, research platforms, and custom applications. No complex software installation required on your vehicle.

Proven Technology

Built on cutting-edge computer vision research, including ORB-SLAM3 visual SLAM and deep learning-based stereo matching trained on the UWStereo underwater dataset.
